        \item One can obtain \textbf{depth estimation} from vision via
        \vspace{-4pt}
        \begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
            \setlength\itemsep{0pt}
            \item \textbf{Stereo} (using 2 or more images)
            \item Structure From Motion \textbf{(SFM)} based on \textit{geometric triangulation} to get pointcloud Reconstruction
            \item Monocular depth estimation via \textbf{ML} networks
